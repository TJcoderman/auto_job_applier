## Partner Playbook

### Target Segments
- **Boutique staffing agencies** – offer a co-branded automation pod for premium candidates.
- **Bootcamps & universities** – include Agentic Job Hunter in career services bundles with alumni tracking.
- **Enterprise talent teams** – deploy for hard-to-fill roles where internal sourcing bandwidth is limited.

### Value Proposition
1. **Speed** – autonomous scouting + tailored résumé delivery reduces time-to-first interview.
2. **Quality** – fit scoring and recruiter feedback loops prioritise actionable leads.
3. **Compliance** – audit trails, manual review modes, and role-based access keep talent ops safe.

### Integration Checklist
- SSO & SCIM integration for enterprise control.
- Webhooks / outbound notifications (Slack, Teams, email).
- Per-partner analytics dashboard with funnel metrics.
- White-label themes and optional CRM/ATS connectors.

### Commercial Options
| Package | Pricing Model | Highlights |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Pro Operator | $99 per seat / month | Unlimited runs, premium support, notification packs |
| Talent Pods | $1,499 / pod / month | Multi-seat access, SLA, custom automation recipes |
| White-Label Partner | Revenue share | Co-branded UI, dedicated success manager, partner APIs |

### Success Metrics
- Interview rate uplift vs. manual process.
- Time saved per application cycle.
- Revenue share or placement fees attributed to agent runs.
- User NPS / satisfaction from weekly check-ins.
